Google Webmaster tools now on iGoogle homepage!

Fri, Feb 29, 2008 Just noticed a new feature on Google webmaster tools. Now we can get important information directly on iGoogle homepage. Adding this gadget is very easy. Simply log in to webmaster tools and under tools there is a new option called Gadgets. Thank you Google and keep coming up with cool stuff like this! Click the button "Add an iGoogle Webmaster Tools Homepage" and you are done! Now, some neat statistics is available right here on my iGoogle Homepage
